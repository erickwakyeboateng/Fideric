Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our products and services.

How long does the Shito last?
Our Shito is slow-cooked with natural preservatives (oil and spices). It can last up to 6 months when stored in a cool, dry place and used with a clean, dry spoon. Refrigeration extends freshness.
Where do you deliver?
We deliver across Accra and Tema. Delivery fees vary based on your location. We also offer bulk shipping to other regions and international orders upon special request.
How do I cook the frozen spring rolls?
Fry them directly from the freezer in hot oil until golden brown (approx 5-7 mins). Do not thaw them first as this may make the pastry soggy.
